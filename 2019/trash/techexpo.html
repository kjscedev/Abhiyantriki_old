A display of the most innovative technology from around the country, ranging from high-speed drones to drag-and-drop robot arms, the Tech Expo is the most eye-catching event of Abhiyantriki. Having collaborated with big names like DRDO, BARC, ISRO, DAE and many more, the tech expo will surely ignite the fire of science and engineering in your budding mind!

MINI HUMANOID ROBOT

The Mini Humanoid Robot is a marvel of modern day engineering. It stands 14" inches tall and consists of 16 servo motors working in tandem to mimic human movement. The Robot can easily dance, sing, and walk. It can also perform other activities which include yoga and swimming. Do not miss this once in a lifetime chance to catch the Mini Humanoid Robot in action only at Abhiyantriki 2018!

PREKSH

Preksh is India's No.1 AR/VR company delivering Visual Marketing Solution (VMS) to businesses across education,healthcare, retail and other sectors. Their patented technology provides an immersive experience to users and enables businesses to communicate their brand story in a unique way.

Tetra EXi (Ostrich Mobility)

This sleek and smart new generation powered wheelchair with revolutionary Split Frame Chassis (SFC) from Ostrich Mobility makes your personal mobility look easier and elegant. This indoor/outdoor mobility equipment provides excellent maneuverability, great comfort, and stability.

JASPER

Bringing to you the Jasper Robot, a friendly neighbourhood delivery robot inspired by Amazon delivery bot. It is a bluetooth controlled semi autonomous robot with the capacity to carry 50+ water bottles. Catch this robot made by young minds at Abhiyantriki 2019.

MELTING MINTS

Melting Mints is a leading provider of 3D content-to-print solutions including 3D printers, print materials on-demand and custom parts for professionals and consumers alike. Catch them live with their 3D printing at Abhiyantriki 2019.

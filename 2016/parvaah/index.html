About Parvaah

Parvaah, is the social initiative of K. J. Somaiya College of Engineering. It is inspired by the idea of giving back to the society. Parvaah was started with a mission to send a message with a social cause through festivals and events. Each year KJSCE undertakes social initiatives based on relevant issues in the society. Abhiyantriki having a diverse crowd and a large reach is an ideal platform for promoting social causes. It created awareness amongst the 4000+ students that attended Symphony, and thousands others in various colleges. Parvaah promises to be a platform to reach out to the mass and bring in the change.

Beautification of Ghatkopar Station

In this event, team parvaah will be cleaning and beautifying Ghatkopar station. The drive is jointly organised and managed by Mumbai First and Making A Difference Foundation as a part of the #HamaraStationHamariShaan initiative. Hamara Station Hamari Shaan is the next big step in beautifying the city of dreams. This initiative is backed by Maharashtra CM Devendra Fadnavis, Ratan N. Tata and Amitabh Bachchan. Ghatkopar station will be cleaned and the walls along with the staircase will be painted to raise awareness about social issues. Each one of us should have a sense of responsibility towards the society to make a difference by our active involvement and here’s our chance to make that happen, so let’s come together and be the change!

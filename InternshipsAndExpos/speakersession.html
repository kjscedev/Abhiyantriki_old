Dr. Jayant Narlikar

We are very proud to announce the very first speaker and a very unique speaker session for Abhiyantriki 2020- Dr Jayant Narlikar! Dr Narlikar and Indian Astrophysicist at IUCAA and has been the name behind the development of the conformal gravity, Hoyle-Narlikar theory, alongside Sir Fred Hoyle. His accomplishments are uncountable on one’s fingers, going from Padma Bhushan, Vibhushan, the Adams Prize to the Prix Jules Janssen! Dr Narlikar will speak on the topic: “Why I became a Scientist”

Arogyaswami Paulraj

Father of MIMO(Multiple Input Multiple Output), and 4G mobile networks! Founding father of three wireless technology companies in Silicon Valley between 1998 to 2014, which were later acquired by Intel, Broadcom, and HPE. Emeritus Professor at Stanford, bestowed with various awards like US Govt. National Inventors Hall of Fame, Marconi Prize, IEEE Alexander Graham Bell Medal, and Friendship Award (PR China Govt.). Proudly presenting to you our next speaker, Padma Bhushan Arogyaswami Paulraj. Join us for his live session on the topic: " Information, Communication & Machine Learning- The Next 25 years."
